The Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Project seek to improve access by rural populations to safe potable water and to support implementation of a program to supply water to rural populations.


Water Facilities

Clean drinking water must be available to all. With that as motto, we invest in large and small scale water facilities in the villages.


Sanitation Facilities

We invest in sanitation facilities as our contribution towards cleanliness.


Participatory Approach

There is no power for change greater than a community discovering what it cares about.
